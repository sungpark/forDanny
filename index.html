Company History

CTL Medical Corporation was established in 2015 by Daniel Chon, former President & CEO of AccelSPINE, with the vision of creating a fully integrated, industry leading, global medical device design, development and manufacturing company.

With that vision in mind, CTL Medical Corporation assembled a world class executive team, bringing together some of the industry’s most exceptional talent. That assembly of talent helped the company realize the recent acquisitions of both RF Precision Manufacturing, a Dallas based medical device manufacturer, and AccelSPINE, a nationally recognized medical device developer.

This combination of manufacturing and development resources further extends CTL Medical Corporation’s position as a leader in medical device design, manufacturing and service.

DEGAS™

Degas™ Anterior Cervical Plate System oﬀers a low proﬁle ACDF plate with one-step screw blocking mechanisms that provide a streamlined ACDF procedural experience. The plate’s low proﬁle and narrow width allows for smaller soft tissue incision and retraction which may reduce incident of post-operative dysphagia. The plate has large window for easy visualization of bone graft and end plates that helps in proper plate and screw positioning.

  • Large Graft Window: Enhances post operative graft visualization
  • One-Step Self-Blocking Mechanism: Eliminates additional steps required to block a screw after its insertion
  • Low Profile Design: Helps reduce soft tissue retraction during placement and decrease post operative soft tissue irritation

Van Gogh™

Van Gogh™ Anterior Cervical Plate System oﬀers a low proﬁle ACDF plate, robust screws, and intuitive instruments that are designed to provide a safe and streamlined ACDF procedural experience. The plate’s narrow width minimizes soft tissue incision and retraction, which may reduce post-operative discomfort. The various screw angulation and purchase oﬀerings provide secure and safe plate stabilization options while securing the bone graft in place.

  • Large Graft Window: Enhances post operative visualization
  • Positive Feedback Blocking Mechanism: Provides visual and tactile feedback following screw insertion
  • Low Profile Design: Helps reduce soft tissue retraction during placement and decrease post operative soft tissue irritation

Matisse™

Matisse™ ACIF PEEK Cage is a cervical interbody fusion device made from radiolucent polymer that is available in various footprints, heights, and lordotic angles in order to accommodate a wide range of patient anatomies and surgeon preferences. Matisse™ ACIF Cage system instruments allow for streamlined implant delivery during anterior cervical fusion procedures.

  • Large Graft Window: Provides large window for maximum graft volume
  • Radiographic Markers: Provides visual feedback for proper placement
  • Anatomic Designs: Accommodate various patient anatomies while resisting migration

Picasso™ II

Picasso™ II Percutaneous Polyaxial Screws are designed with simplicity in mind. Made for true percutaneous implantation, they allow for complete pedicle stabilization while minimizing tissue damage. The polyaxial screw head is uniquely integrated with a percutaneous tower, allowing up to 30mm of reduction with the same locking set screw for truly streamlined MIS approach.

  • Simple Design: Integrated percutaneous reduction tower and single step locking set screw allow efficient implant installation
  • ACME Thread Locking Set Screw: Eliminates cross-threading while set screw tightening
  • Double Lead Thread: Permits quick and precise screw advancement

Raphael™

Raphael™ Pedicle Screw System consists of various rods, multi-application polyaxial screws, and locking set screws that provide a simple and secure top-loading, rigid ﬁxation. It provides a wide variety of ergonomically designed instruments to ensure exact placement of implants without compromising construct integrity.

  • Simple Design: Integrated percutaneous reduction tower and single step locking set screw allow efficient implant installation
  • ACME Thread Locking Set Screw: Eliminates cross-threading while set screw tightening
  • Double Lead Thread: Permits quick and precise screw advancement

Cezanne™

Cezanne™ TLIF PEEK Cages are transforaminal lumbar interbody fusion implants. In order to accommodate a wide range of patient anatomies and surgeon preferences these implants are available with a curved shape and a self-distracting tip for ease of insertion, radiopaque markers for optimal placement isualization, aggressive serrations to ensure endplate ﬁxation, and a large graft window for ample surface area to promote fusion.

  • Large Graft Window: Provides large window for maximum graft volume
  • Radiographic Markers: Provides visual feedback for proper implant placement
  • Curved Footprint: Accommodates varied patient anatomies
